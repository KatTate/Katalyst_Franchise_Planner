### The "Gurple" Story

The brand guidelines identify **Grayish Purple** ("Gurple") as Katalyst's signature color, representing "insightful solutions" and "smart thinking." In the Performance Platform, this maps to `kat-mystical` (#A9A2AA).

**When to use Gurple:**
- Info icons and tooltips (hover help)
- Insight panels and explanatory content
- "Pro tip" or "Did you know?" callouts
- Settings descriptions and documentation hints
- Audit log and history views (institutional knowledge)

**CSS classes:** `bg-kat-mystical/20`, `border-kat-mystical/30`, `text-kat-mystical` (for headings on light backgrounds)

**Accessibility guidelines:**
- **Info icons**: Use `text-kat-gauntlet` or `text-kat-charcoal` for the default state (meets 3:1+ contrast). Add `hover:text-kat-mystical` to reveal the brand color on interaction.
- **Info panels**: Use `bg-kat-mystical/20 border-kat-mystical/30` for the container, with `text-kat-mystical` for panel headings.
- **Tooltips**: Dark backgrounds (`bg-kat-black`) with white text for 4.5:1 contrast. Add `border-kat-mystical/30` accent to tie in the brand color.

## Brand Voice & Copy

The Katalyst brand voice is **Vibrant, Quick-witted, and Authentic**.

### Tone Guidelines

| Katalyst IS | Katalyst is SOMETIMES | Katalyst is NEVER |
|-------------|----------------------|-------------------|
| Vibrant | Challenging | Intense |
| Quick-witted | Funny | Crass |
| Authentic | Serious | Mean-spirited |

### Words to Use
Activate, elevate, collaborative, enhance, propel, accelerate, launch, inspire, partner, grow, create, build, connect

### Words to Avoid
- "Best" or other non-provable descriptors
- "Synergy" or marketing buzzwords
- Overly technical jargon

### Empty States & Error Messages

Write with warmth and encouragement:

| Instead of... | Try... |
|---------------|--------|
| "No data available" | "Nothing here yet. Check back soon!" |
| "Error occurred" | "That didn't work. Let's try again." |
| "No items found" | "All clear! Nothing to show right now." |
| "Failed to load" | "We hit a snag. No worries, we can fix this." |

### Module Subtitles

Keep subtitles short, benefit-focused, and action-oriented:
- Dashboard: "The pulse of Katalyst. Everything that matters, right now."
- Core Capital: "Your cash clarity dashboard. Know where you stand, instantly."
- Payroll: "Build your dream team. See the real cost before you commit."
- Revenue: "Turn capacity into opportunity. Plan smarter, grow faster."
